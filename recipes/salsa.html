Tasty Orange Hot Salsa

Description

I made this salsa once when I couldn't find my normal chiles and ended up loving it.

Ingredients

  • 4 roma tomatoes
  • 1 guajillo chile
  • 1 ancho chile
  • 1 serrano chile (optional for extra spice)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ¼ white onion
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a small cooking pot, coating the bottom. Put on med-high heat.
  2. Cut tomatoes into quarters. De-stem and rinse the seeds from the chiles. Peel garlic clove and cut in quarters.
  3. Add Tomatoes, chiles, garlic, onion and salt to pot. Cook for 8 minutes stirring frequently. Turn on an exhaust fan so your whole family doesn't start coughing and yelling at you.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of water, reduce to med heat, stir frequently for 5 more minutes.
  5. Pour all ingredients into a blender. Add cilantro and 1 cup of ice cubes.
  6. Blend on med-high until liquid consistency.
  7. Taste, add salt if needed.
  8. Enjoy on tacos, with chips, or anything really.
  9. Refrigerate when you can't eat anymore. Rest well knowing that after spending a day in the fridge it tastes even better.
